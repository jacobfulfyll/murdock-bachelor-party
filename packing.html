What To Pack

0/0 packed

Essentials

  • Valid ID / driver's license
  • Cash (for bars, tips, poker)
  • Phone charger / portable battery
  • Sunglasses
  • Sunscreen (Utah sun is no joke at altitude)

Outdoor / Active

  • Hiking shoes or sturdy sneakers
  • Athletic shorts / moisture-wicking clothes
  • Swimsuit (lake day Saturday)
  • Towel
  • Hat / cap
  • Light jacket (mountain evenings get cool)
  • Water bottle

Going Out

  • Nice-ish dinner outfit (Friday at Repeal)
  • Bar crawl clothes (Saturday Park City)
  • Comfortable walking shoes for downtown

Airbnb Hangout

  • Comfy clothes / sweats
  • Flip flops or slides
  • Toiletries
  • Any medications
  • Sleeping bag or pillow if you're picky
