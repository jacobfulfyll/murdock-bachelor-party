Styling Guide
Beautifully designed nature websites utilize calming color palettes (rich greens, earthy tones), high-quality organic imagery, and seamless animations to evoke environmental immersion. Top examples include The Nature Conservancy, The Scott Resort (using deep green, luxury aesthetics), 350.org (using vibrant, impactful photos), and Brother Earth (featuring nature illustrations).

Key Elements of Nature-Inspired Design:
Color Palette: Dominated by deep greens, muted earth tones, and sunny accents.
Imagery: High-definition photographs of landscapes, forests, and wildlife.
Layout: Clean, spacious, and often minimalist to allow natural elements to stand out.
Interactive Elements: Subtle animations, such as moving backgrounds or scrolling effects, simulate natural environments (e.g., rustling leaves, flowing water).
Focus: Often used for sustainability, non-profit, tourism, and outdoor lifestyle brands.
